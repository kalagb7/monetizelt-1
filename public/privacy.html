1. Introduction

Welcome to Monetizelt (“we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and relevant U.S. federal and state regulations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Who We Are

We provide a digital platform that allows users (“Sellers”) to upload and sell ephemeral digital videos and other digital goods through secure temporary links.

3. Information We Collect

3.1. Account Information

Name, email address, country of residence, and password.

Payment details (PayPal email, transaction IDs).

Identification data if required for tax or verification (e.g., W-9, W-8BEN).

3.2. Transaction Data

Product purchases, sales history, pricing, and withdrawal logs.

Buyer’s email (for purchase delivery only).

PayPal transaction metadata.

3.3. Technical Data

Device information, IP address, browser type, operating system.

Session logs, cookies, and analytics data.

3.4. Communication Data

Messages sent to our support team.

Feedback, bug reports, or content reports.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Operate and manage the Monetizelt platform.
  2. Process and verify transactions via PayPal.
  3. Deliver purchased digital products securely.
  4. Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.
  5. Comply with applicable laws and tax regulations.
  6. Provide user support and improve services.
  7. Send essential updates about your account or changes to our policies.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal bases for processing your data are:

Contractual necessity: To provide our platform and fulfill transactions.

Legal obligation: To comply with anti-fraud, financial, and tax laws.

Legitimate interest: To maintain and improve platform security.

Consent: For optional cookies or promotional communications.

6. Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary and with trusted partners:

PayPal — for payment processing.

Google Cloud Platform (GCP) — for encrypted data hosting.

Analytics providers — for traffic and performance metrics.

Legal authorities — if required by law or subpoena.

Each partner operates under its own strict privacy policies and data protection standards.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

Maintain session security and authentication;

Measure site traffic and performance;

Improve usability and user experience.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not function properly without cookies.

8. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform (U.S. servers). We apply:

AES-256 encryption for stored data;

HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit;

Access restrictions and regular security audits.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill our obligations or as required by law (e.g., financial and tax records).

9. Data Retention

Your account data is kept while your account is active.

When deleted, we:

Retain minimal transaction records for legal/tax purposes (up to 7 years).

Permanently erase all other identifiable data.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access the data we hold about you;

Request correction or deletion;

Object to certain processing activities;

Request data portability;

Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@monetizelt.com.

11. Children’s Privacy

Monetizelt is not intended for individuals under 18 years old.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred and processed outside your country of residence, including the United States.

All transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards under GDPR and other international data protection frameworks.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

14. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or on our website before the changes take effect.
